About Us

Himachal Pradesh Technical University (HPTU) was established by the government of Himachal Pradesh. The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various technical fields.

Courses Offered

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)
  • Master of Technology (M.Tech)
  • Doctoral Programs (Ph.D.)
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
